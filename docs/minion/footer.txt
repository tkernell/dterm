




Would you like to propose a change? Type:

    minion resolver
  
    Then hit <enter>

You will be asked a series of questions about your desired change.
This will initiate a transaction that submits the change to the DAO.
Make sure you are on the ROPSTEN testnet!

Note: A DAO member must sponsor your proposal before members vote on it.
Note 2: Hit CTRL+D to cancel
